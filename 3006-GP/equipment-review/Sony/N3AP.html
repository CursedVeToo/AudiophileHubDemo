Sony® N3AP


In a market where smaller is often seen as more attractive, the earpieces on the Sony® XBA-3iP earphones aren’t quite as slender as on comparable models from Klipsch or Beyerdynamic, but that’s because they accommodate the triple balanced armature drivers complete with tweeter and woofer. Call quality is crystal clear, as is the sound produced when spinning tunes.
