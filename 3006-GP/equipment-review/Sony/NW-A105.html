Sony® NW-A105


If you're looking for a dedicated audio player with Android on it, get the Walkman® NW-A105. It's wonderful. Buy a MicroSD card so you can use the device to its full potential. Other DAPs at the time of my purchase (2020), while perhaps cheaper, don't offer the SD card option or Android or the excellent EQ features. Battery life when using WiFi isn't great (4-6 hours depending on your usage). Offline the device lasts at least 10 hours.
