Beyerdynamic® T1 3rd-Generation


The Beyerdynamic® T1 3rd Generation are good, premium headphones for neutral sound. They have a slightly warm sound profile and have a great build quality. They're comfortable to wear for a while but may feel a bit tight for some. By design, they don’t isolate any noise and are very leaky, making them unsuitable for commuting. These headphones are only really suitable for their intended usage, neutral sound.
